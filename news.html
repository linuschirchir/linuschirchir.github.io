This page highlights recent professional milestones, research activities, conference presentations, publications, awards, funding, and other updates from my work in population data science, administrative data research, and Trusted Research Environments (TREs).

November 2026

🎤 Invited to Present FRAME-DS at the CaSDaR Hybrid Data Steward Conference 2026

Invited to present FRAME-DS: Framework for Recognising Applied Data Stewardship Within TRE Workforces at the CaSDaR Hybrid Data Steward Conference 2026 at The Midland, Manchester, on 17–18 November 2026. The conference brings together data stewards, researchers, research technical professionals, and research infrastructure leaders from across the UK to share best practice, showcase funded projects, and shape the future of data stewardship.

July 2026

🎤 Selected as a Panellist for the Global Talent Research & Academia Summit 2026

Selected as a panellist for the inaugural Global Talent Research & Academia Summit 2026 at the University Park Campus, University of Nottingham on 11 July 2026. The summit brings together UK Global Talent Visa recipients from across research and academia to share their journeys, experiences, and contributions to research and innovation in the UK.

June 2026

📢 Presented FRAME-DS to the SDAP Community

Presented FRAME-DS (Framework for Recognising Applied Data Stewardship Within TRE Workforces to the Secure Data Access Professionals (SDAP) community. The presentation introduced the project and explored how applied data stewardship can be recognised, supported, and strengthened across Trusted Research Environments (TREs) and Safe Havens.

May 2026

🌍 UKRI-Endorsed Global Talent Recipient

Received endorsement from UK Research and Innovation (UKRI) under the Global Talent Endorsed Funder route in recognition of contributions to UKRI-funded research and innovation through Administrative Data Research UK (ADR UK) and Research Data Scotland, supporting the secure and responsible use of administrative data for research and public benefit.

April 2026

📈 Achieved RSS Data Science Professional Status

Awarded Data Science Professional status by the Royal Statistical Society (RSS), recognising professional competence and experience in data science and applied analytics.

March 2026

💷 Awarded UKRI CaSDaR Funding as Principal Investigator

Awarded £64,508.43 as Principal Investigator for FRAME-DS: Framework for Recognising Applied Data Stewardship Within TRE Workforces – A Collaborative, Cross-Organisation Pilot through the UKRI-funded Careers and Skills for Data-Driven Research (CaSDaR) Network+ Research Technical Professional Call. FRAME-DS was one of four projects selected for funding from 24 applications submitted to the competitive £70,000 funding stream, as announced by the CaSDaR Network+. Delivered collaboratively by Research Data Scotland and Electronic Data Research and Innovation Service (eDRIS) at Public Health Scotland, the project explores how applied data stewardship can be recognised, supported, and strengthened across TREs.

December 2024

📊 Awarded RSS Data Analyst Status

Awarded Data Analyst status by the Royal Statistical Society.

September 2024

🔬 Joined Research Data Scotland

Joined Research Data Scotland as a Data Analyst, supporting the preparation, documentation, governance, and reuse of administrative datasets for research within Scotland’s National Safe Haven. My work focuses on metadata, synthetic data, reproducible analytical pipelines, and research data infrastructure that support population and social science research.

September 2023

🏥 Joined the University of Edinburgh

Joined the University of Edinburgh as a Health Data Analyst, developing reproducible R and SQL pipelines for linked NHS datasets and supporting research into patient outcomes and healthcare services. I also developed interactive dashboards and contributed to data governance, metadata standardisation, and research data management activities.

July 2023

🎓 Graduated with an MSc in Health Data Science

Graduated from Swansea University with an MSc in Health Data Science. The programme strengthened my interests in population data science, administrative data research, machine learning, and reproducible analytics. My dissertation applied machine learning methods to investigate the prediction of early-stage Alzheimer’s disease using the OASIS-3 dataset.

January 2022

🚀 Began MSc in Health Data Science

Started the MSc in Health Data Science at Swansea University, marking my transition into population data science, administrative data research, machine learning, and advanced statistical analysis. The programme provided training in health informatics, epidemiology, statistics, machine learning, and population data science, laying the foundation for my subsequent work in Trusted Research Environments, synthetic data, metadata, and research data infrastructure.
